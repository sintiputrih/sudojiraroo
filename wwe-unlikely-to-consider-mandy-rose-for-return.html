WWE Unlikely to Consider Mandy Rose for Return

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

WWE fired Mandy Rose right after she dropped the NXT Women’s Title. She experienced a resurgence in NXT during that run, but she also started a NSFW premium content paywall that caused a lot of trouble and broke a clause in her contract. That meant she had to go, but some fans are still burning a candle for her eventual return.

Mandy Rose’s name still makes headlines, along with stories about her time in WWE. It is very unlikely at this point that she will have a chance to tell more stories in the company unless something drastic changes.

Ringside News reached out to a tenured member of the team to inquire about any interest in bringing Mandy Rose back to WWE. We were told that her return to the company isn’t likely.

We were told that they never hear Mandy Rose’s name come up. Also, there is a huge doubt that she will come up again in creative discussions, or as a possibility to bring her back.

You can never say never in WWE, and that statement is always true, but Mandy Rose has done a lot with her NSFW platform since her WWE departure. It is unlikely that the company will have her back at this point, especially after she stepped up her game on OnlyFans.

Mandy Rose is doing just fine. She’s making ton of money with her premium content, and she seems to be living her best life. WWE has obviously moved on from her as NXT Women’s Champion, and her Toxic Attraction stablemates, Jacy Jayne and Gigi Dolin, have also found new stories in NXT.

Only time will tell if Mandy Rose returns to WWE for a reunion or something else down the line. At this point, a full-fledged return doesn’t seem to be in the cards at all.
