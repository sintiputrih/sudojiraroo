Daneboe is an American social media personality, filmmaker, singer, and actor. Daneboe is popular for his web series ‘The Annoying Orange’ and the spin-off television series ‘The High Fructose Adventures of Annoying Orange.

Early Life and Childhood

Daneboe was born on 28th September 1979 and is currently 44 years old. His full name is Dane Willard Boedigheimer and his zodiac sign is Libra. Likewise, Daneboe was born in Itasca, Minnesota, United States of America, and holds American citizenship. Similarly, talking about his ethnicity and religion, he belongs to the Caucasian ethnic group and follows Christianity. Likewise, regarding his family background, Daneboe is the son of a roofer and has one brother whose name is Luke Boedigheimer.

Talking about his childhood, he began making home videos as a teen. Likewise, his first camera was his parents’ 8mm video camera which he got for Christmas at the age of 12. Similarly, he used to spend his entire day making short videos with his brother. They used to sit in the room for hours editing videos with two VCRs and making crappy stories. Likewise, his first-ever movie was in collaboration with his little brother. He made a movie ‘Pugzilla featuring a toy barnyard set and some Matchbox cars which were damaged by his brother’s dog.

Furthermore, talking about his educational background, he studied filmmaking at the Minnesota State University Moorhead from the year 1998-2003. Likewise, he majored in speech communication with an emphasis on film studies which became the preparation phase for his ‘Annoying Orange’ career. Similarly, he worked at the one-hour photo lab during his college days. Likewise, one of my final projects in college is a full-length feature film titled ‘Trash TV’. This show was forecasted at the Fargo Theatre where more than 200 people came to watch the movie.

Daneboe – Professional Career

After graduating college, he along with his girlfriend moved to Bakersfield, California, and shifted to Los Angeles in December 2005. Here both of them worked as production assistants for MTV’s Pimp My Ride series. Moreover, after completing this show, he decided to stop working on television and started his creation. Likewise, on 14th February 2005, he founded the company ‘Gagfilms’.

Following this, in the year 2006, he became a freelance filmmaker in January and opened two YouTube channels called ‘Daneboe’ and ‘Gagfilms’. Similarly, he started the former channel on 25th March 2006, and has 1.55 million subscribers now. Likewise, he opened another channel ‘GagFilms’ along with other two fellow YouTubers @aaronmassey and @bobjenz. They joined this channel on 2nd November 2007 and have 142k subscribers in it.

Initially, it was only meant to be the Annoying Orange video on YouTube, but with its growing popularity, he made a full-time series after its fourth episode. Similarly, after the success of this series, he created a channel ‘realannoyingorange’ which was fully dedicated to the Annoying Orange series.

Moreover, the success of this series received the attention of the Fargo Advertising Agency H2M which created its own ‘Talking Orange’ for its ad campaign for the North Dakota Department of Transportation.
