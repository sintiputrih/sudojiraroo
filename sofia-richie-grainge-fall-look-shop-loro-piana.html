Sofia Richie Grainge Does Easy Autumn Elegance at Loro Piana Event

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, April 28, 2024

Sofia Richie Grainge’s easy, elegant style just got the Loro Piana treatment.

The It girl joined the maison for an intimate dinner at a private villa on Malibu’s Carbon Beach on Tuesday evening to celebrate its Fall 2023 Cocooning collection, which now includes menswear.

The cozy fall drop features raw cashmere knits in deep earth tones and bright creams, as well as flowy dresses, fuzzy sweaters, and comfy pants to lounge in.

For the celebration, Richie Grainge put together a look from the brand that perfectly mirrored her casually sophisticated aesthetic. The central piece was an asymmetric white wool skirt, which Richie Grainge paired with a white top, and over it layered a cropped brown leather bomber jacket with silver buttons. To match, she carried a brown leather Loro Piana extra pocket pouch L19, also with silver hardware, and wore pointy heels in a glossy brown. The natural palette continued in her glam, as Richie Grainge—the beauty director at Nudestix—wore bronzy makeup and had her dirty-blonde hair styled in easy waves.

Jojo Korsh/BFA.com//BFA

To celebrate Loro Piana’s dreamy new collection, guests at the event enjoyed a meditative sound bath, followed by a cocktail hour with a fireside live performance by singer Aidan Bissett, and then a seated dinner by chef Yann Nury.

Other notable attendees in attendance included Felix Mallard, Gavin Casalegno, Madelaine Petsch, Patrick Schwarzenegger, Olivia Holt, Rainey Qualley, Jasmine Tookes, Dree Hemingway, Alika Mock, Michael February, Larsen Thompson, Olivia Perez, CeCe Vu, Mandy Madden Kelley, Liat Baruch, Mimi Cuttrell, and Rebecca Ramsey.
