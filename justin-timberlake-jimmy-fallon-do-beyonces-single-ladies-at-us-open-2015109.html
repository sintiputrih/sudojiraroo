Justin Timberlake, Jimmy Fallon Do Beyonce's Single Ladies at Us Open

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, February 7, 2024

Drunk in love or simply flawless? Justin Timberlake shared new photos of his 5-month-old son, Silas, and brought back some rap history to The Tonight Show on Wednesday, Sept. 9, but boy, oh boy, that was nothing compared to his dancing chops on display at the US Open. The “Mirrors” singer and host Jimmy Fallon crushed the competition when they shook their bums to Beyonce’s “Single Ladies (Put a Ring on It)” from their seats.

The longtime pals were prompted to put down their beers and entertain the crowd when Queen Bey’s 2008 smash came on the PA system at the Arthur Ashe Stadium in Queens, NYC in the middle of a match between Roger Federer and Richard Gasquet. The two flashed their wedding ring hands in the Emirates Airlines suite and mimicked the singer’s iconic choreography. Fallon, 40, turned red while Timberlake, 34, tried his best to not crack a smile.

Of course, Timberlake had a lot of practice. Back in 2008, the former boybander pretended to be one of Bey’s backup dancers during the same song as one of his Saturday Night Live hosting skits. He wore the same music video leotard and was joined by Andy Samberg and Bobby Moynihan.
