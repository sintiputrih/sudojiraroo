Career

Wooden started his acting career when he was 3 years old, initially, he appeared on the Broadcast television network ‘Court TV’. After his fame, he got a chance to work on different commercials for PBS Kids and Nickelodeon Channel.

In the year, 2008 he debuted, Step Up 2: The Streets in the role of Charlie. The movie was based on the life of two dancers with different backgrounds.

After that, he was cast in different TV  shows like ‘ Instant Mom’  and ‘The Battery’s Down’ in the years 2009 and 2014 respectively.

One of his famous roles was ‘Sheldon’ in the show ‘Splitting Adam in 2015. Soon he participated in different projects like Fantasy Life in 2015, Life in Pieces, LAPD African Cops, Life in Pieces, Bunk’d, and Criminal Minds in the same year 2016.

However, he was recognized only after the TV series Liv and Maddie as Finch from 2016 to 2017. Similarly, he was spotted in American Housewife as Eyo. He was highly appreciated for his role.

During the year between 2018 to 2019, he was again featured as the lead in Knight Squad where he was portrayed as Warwick who is a member of Phoenix Squad and training to be a knight at the knight school.

Later he appeared in Holiday Rush as Jamal Williams which was featured on Netflix. Apart from that, he has also appeared in different TV shows.
