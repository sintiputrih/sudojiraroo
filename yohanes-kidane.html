All About Netflix’s Software Engineer Yohanes Kidane

Yohanes Kidane (age 22, May 5, 2001 – August 14, 2023) a software engineer at Netflix, has been missing since Monday evening. Yohanes had recently started his career at Netflix after graduating from Cornell University in May.

His older brother, Yosief Kidane, shared that Yohanes took an Uber from his apartment building at 28 N 4th Street on August 14, 2023, around 7:15 pm. He was last seen wearing gray sweatpants, a black hoodie, and black shoes.

Yosief Kidane, along with their father, flew from New York to the Bay Area to search for Yohanes. Surveillance footage from his apartment building captured Yohanes getting into a black Toyota Sedan with an Uber sticker.

He mentioned to colleagues that he was headed to San Francisco, possibly to meet a friend. However, his phone’s tracker has remained static near the Golden Gate Bridge since Monday evening.

The circumstances surrounding Yohanes’ disappearance raise concerns. His phone, wallet, and backpack were discovered south of the Golden Gate Bridge in a public area near the welcome center and coffee shop.

A commuter discovered his phone and wallet on a grassy hill and turned them into the Marin California Highway Patrol’s office. The police also located his backpack near the Golden Gate Bridge, containing two laptops and personal documents. Yohanes’ family reported the case to the San Jose police, and they are actively seeking information to locate him.
