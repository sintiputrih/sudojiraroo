Britney Spears Works Major Side Boob in Workout Outfit: Photo

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, May 11, 2024

Major side boob—or just boob boob!

Britney Spears hit the streets of Westlake Village, Calif., on Wednesday, Sept. 2, rocking quite the revealing workout ensemble.

The pop princess, 33, who's been busy jetting back and forth to Las Vegas for her hugely successful Piece of Me show, exited the local gym sporting a white tank top with deeply cut arm openings, putting a generous portion of her bubblegum-hued sports bra on full display. She also wore plaid shorts, neon sneakers, and tortoiseshell sunglasses.
