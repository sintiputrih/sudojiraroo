Chicago Cubs Paid Tribute To Bray Wyatt On WWE Night

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

The Chicago Cubs paid tribute to Bray Wyatt earlier this week at WWE Night.

WWE Night was hosted at Wrigley Field on Monday, August 28, when the Cubs hosted the Brewers. Fans who purchased a special WWE ticket to the event received a Clark Hogan bobblehead when they arrived at the stadium.

WWE Superstars Big E and Grayson Waller were on hand for the festivities and appeared in front of the crowd during the seventh-inning stretch. The Cubs organization also paid tribute to the recently deceased Bray Wyatt at the event on Monday.

Outside of the ballpark, a ring was set up with a rocking chair and lantern placed in the center of it. You can check out a screenshot of the setup in the embedded tweet below.
