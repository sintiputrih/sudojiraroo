The Truth About The Women Robert Downey Jr. Has Dated And Their Massive Net Worths

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, June 22, 2024

During Robert Downey Jr.’s time in the public eye, his life and career have had some wild ups and downs. Thankfully, Downey Jr. has enjoyed a great deal of success over the last several years largely due to his starring role in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Similarly, Downey Jr. is winning in his battle against his addictions and he seems healthy and happy these days.

On top of finding great success in his career and remaining sober for years, it is clear that Robert Downey Jr.’s longstanding marriage plays a huge role in how happy he is. However, like most people, Downey Jr. got involved with several other people before he found his current spouse. When looking back at Downey Jr.’s love life, it is interesting to compare the net worths of the women he has been involved with.

6 Jennifer Jason Leigh’s Net Worth Is $5 Million

In the years since he rose to fame, Robert Downey Jr. has been involved with several women including the first star he is known to have dated, Jennifer Jason Leigh. First made famous when she starred in Fast Times at Ridgemont High, Leigh went on to star in a long list of other films including Backdraft, Single White Female, and The Hateful Eight. Still an extremely talented and in high demand actor to this day, Leigh is currently worth $5 million according to celebritynetworth.com.

5 Winona Ryder’s Net Worth Is $18 Million

Since Robert Downey Jr. has been famous since the late-‘80s and his life has been tumultuous at times, it shouldn’t come as a surprise to anyone that there have been many rumors about him. For example, Downey Jr.’s love life has been the subject of gossip including rumors that he briefly dated Winona Ryder in 1991. At the height of Ryder’s career, she was in the running for almost every highly sought-after role that was to be played by a young woman. After becoming a major movie star, Ryder’s career went on a downhill slide for several years but once Stranger Things became a sensation, it rebounded. As a result of her immense acting talent and having a will strong enough to weather Hollywood for years, Ryder is worth $18 million according to celebritynetworth.com.

4 Susan Downey’s Net Worth Is $20 Million

A few years after Robert Downey Jr. gained notoriety, he married a woman named Deborah Falconer but there are no reliable accounts of her current net worth. On the other hand, a lot more is known about the actor’s second and current wife, Susan Downey. The former co-president of Dark Castle Entertainment and executive vice president of production at Silver Pictures, Susan has always been a formidable person. Currently the co-founder and co-president of the production company Team Downey, Susan is set to continue influencing the film industry for many years to come. As a result of her impressive career and life, Susan Downey is worth $20 million according to celebritynetworth.com.

3 Marisa Tomei’s Net Worth Is $25 Million

In the movie Captain America: Civil War, Robert Downey Jr.’s Tony Stark can be seen flirting with Marisa Tomei’s Aunt May. For people unfamiliar with the actors’ history, that may have seemed unremarkable since Stark is a character who flirts with every attractive woman. Given the fact that Downey Jr. and Tomei briefly dated in 1993, however, flirting on camera must have felt like déjà vu. A truly talented actor, Tomei has starred in many highly successful films including the Spider-Man movies, My Cousin Vinny, and Crazy, Stupid, Love. After surviving in Hollywood for decades, Tomei is worth $25 million according to celebritynetworth.com

2 Calista Flockhart’s Net Worth IS $30 Million

Just like Winona Ryder, it has never been confirmed that Calista Flockhart dated Robert Downey Jr. as their time together has only been rumored. However, it is known that from 1997 until 2002 Flockhart starred in one of the most talked-about shows on television, Ally McBeal. As fans of the show will no doubt remember, Ally McBeal gave Robert Downey Jr. the chance to prove that he could still light up a screen following a stint in prison. On top of headlining Ally McBeal, Flockhart has also starred in shows like Brothers & Sisters as well as Supergirl in the past. Thanks to her roles in those shows and appearing in several movies, Flockhart has a $30 million fortune according to celebritynetworth.com.

1 Sarah Jessica Parker’s Net Worth Is $200 Million

At this point, Sarah Jessica Parker has been married to Matthew Broderick for roughly two and a half decades. However, long before she walked down the aisle, Parker starred in a movie titled Firstborn with Robert Downey Jr. and then they dated from 1984 until 1991. Arguably among the top TV stars of all time, Parker is now best known for her time bringing Carrie Bradshaw to life in the Sex and the City franchise. Since that franchise has an incredibly devoted fanbase, Sarah has been able to cash in on the role so much that Parker is worth $200 million according to celebritynetworth.com.
