Danielle Knox Biography

Danielle Knox is an American Emmy Award-winning journalist. She anchors Good Day Orlando. Before coming to Orlando’s Fox 35, Danielle anchored at WSVN in Miami. Danielle is able to move from news to entertainment with ease, having previously co-hosted the national morning show “The Balancing Act”, seen every weekday morning on Lifetime TV.

Prior to co-hosting “The Balancing Act”, Danielle anchored during the weekends at WGCL in Atlanta. She also anchored for WFOR in Miami, being both a morning and evening anchor. Prior to joining that CBS station, Danielle anchored at WTVJ, the NBC station in Miami. Knox also worked in Hartford, Connecticut, where she was an anchor/reporter at WVIT-TV.
