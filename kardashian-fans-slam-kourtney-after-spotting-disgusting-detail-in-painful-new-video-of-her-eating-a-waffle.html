Kardashian fans slam Kourtney after spotting 'disgusting' detail in 'painful' new video of her eatin

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, April 18, 2024

KARDASHIAN fans have slammed Kourtney for spotting a gross detail in a new video of her eating a waffle.

The Hulu star is seen gingerly cutting up a waffle smothered in maple syrup, with her long feathery sleeves dipping onto the plate.

The video, posted to TikTok is captioned: “Vegan chicken and waffles.”

Fans commenting on the video noticed that her giant sleeves seemed to be in the way of the food on her plate.

“This pained me to watch,” says the caption on the video.

“The sleeves are killing me,” wrote another fan.

Kourtney Kardashian’s blog, Poosh, labels the media mogul as “(mostly, like 95%) vegan.”

The outlet said Kourtney has gone meat-free, and is easing into the lifestyle without strictly holding herself to a specific diet.

“If you’re thinking about becoming vegan, you can make the shift in small waves if that’s easier for you,” the blog reads.

“Start by replacing meat with plant-based options once a week and increase from there.”

Poosh continued: “Kourt admits when she first started, she couldn’t resist and had a baked crab handroll while at dinner during the first few weeks.”

Her husband, Travis Barker has been vegetarian since his teenage years, and switched over to vegan life after he survived a plane crash in 2008.

Aside from trying to eat entirely plant-based, Kourtney Kardashian has been known to remove things like gluten, sugar, and dairy from her diet.

Kourtney also eats a ketogenic diet on-and-off, which consists of low-carb and high-protein meals.

EATING FOR TWO?

Fans took notice that the video doesn’t really show Kourtney eating much of her food.

It comes as some fans speculate that she could be pregnant with her fourth child.

The speculation running rampant, after the reality star was seen out and about grabbing lunch with her husband Travis Barker, 46, and her son Reign, seven.

The reality star wore baggy sweatpants, a graphic baseball tee, and slides for the casual outing.

She recently was seen wearing a baggy shirt while hitting the pool with Travis.

Fans noticed the Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum unusually covered up while jumping into a pool.

In the video posted by Travis, the eldest Kardashian sister wears a black long sleeve shirt and shorts.

The newlyweds hold hands as Travis says, “Ready?”

They then run towards an inflatable slide, headfirst, into the pool while Kourtney lets out a excited scream.

She and Travis have previously spoken about their desire to welcome a baby together, and the recent first season of The Kardashians saw them undergo IVF treatment.
