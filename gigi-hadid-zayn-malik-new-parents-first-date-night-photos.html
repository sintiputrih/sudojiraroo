Gigi Hadid &amp; Zayn Malik's First Date Night Since Becoming Parents

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, June 26, 2024

With the help of family members, Gigi Hadid and Zayn Malik have managed to have their first date night since becoming parents.

Hadid posted several snaps on her Instagram Stories, giving fans an insight into the couple's lives as new parents. "Mom and dad's first date night," Hadid captioned a photo of their romantic meal. Of their daughter, Hadid wrote, "She's in the other room w Oma but miss her sm lol." The "Oma" referenced in the post is most likely Gigi's mom, Yolanda Hadid.

A source recently told E! News that Gigi, Zayn, and their new baby girl had been staying at the Hadid family farm in Pennsylvania, where Yolanda had been "helping them tremendously... She is their built-in nurse and they are so grateful to have her."

Instagram

Posting another snap taken during the date, Hadid wrote, "date night cheesey [sic] garlic pull apart."

Instagram

Last month, Malik shared news of his daughter's birth on Twitter. He wrote, "Our baby girl is here, healthy & beautiful. To try put into words how I am feeling right now would be an impossible task. The love I feel for this tiny human is beyond my understanding. Grateful to know her, proud to call her mine, & thankful for the life we will have together x."

A source recently told E! News, "Gigi is very tired but is already an amazing mom... The first couple of weeks have been a hard transition but she hasn't complained and is very elated to be a mom."
