Kehlani aka Kehlani Ashley Parrish is an American dancer, songwriter, and singer. As of 2023, Kehlani’s net worth is $6 million. She rose to fame after becoming a member of the famous teen group named Poplyfe. She is in the industry since the year 2009.

In the year 2016, she received a Grammy nomination for her work in You Should Be Here for Best Urban Contemporary Album. She majorly works with the genres neo soul, hip hop, and R&B, and works with the labels HBK, /Atlantic, and Tsunami Mob.

Kehlani Wiki/Biography

Born on 24 April 1995, Kehlani’s age is 28 Years Old as of 2023. She was born and brought up in a lower-middle-class Christian family from Oakland, California, United States. She holds an American nationality and has her belief in Christian religion. Her zodiac sign is Taurus and ethnicity is Cherokee, Filipino, Spanish, Mexican, and African-American Descent.

Kehlani Childhood

She completed her early education at the Oakland School of Arts in Oakland, California, United States. She later enrolled herself at the Julliard School in California, the United States to pursue her dream of being a professional dancer. From the very beginning of her childhood, she was more interested in co-curricular activities rather than studying.
