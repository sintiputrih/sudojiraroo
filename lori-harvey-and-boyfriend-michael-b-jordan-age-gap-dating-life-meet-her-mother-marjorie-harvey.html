Lori Harvey says she and her boyfriend Michael B. Jordan are '4Lifers' in honor of his 35th birthday

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

Influencer Lori Harvey declared on Wednesday that she and her boyfriend Michael B. Jordan are ‘4Lifers’ in a PDA-filled Instastory featuring emojis of a white heart and crossed fingers.

‘Happy birthday to my big baby!’ the 25-year-old adopted daughter of Steve Harvey – who boasts 4.2M Instagram followers – gushed.

‘Can’t wait to see what amazing things this year brings for you my love.’
