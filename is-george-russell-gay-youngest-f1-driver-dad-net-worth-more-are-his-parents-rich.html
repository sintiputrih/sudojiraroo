The youngest F1 driver George Russell, shocked his fans when speculation of him being gay was revealed.

George Russell is a British F1 driver who has been racing for William since 2019. After a glorious 3 years with the team, Russell is set to leave the team when the contract is up.

Is George Russell Gay? Sexuality Explored

George Russell is not at all gay. It was reported in 2020 that he was in a relationship with Carment Montero Mundt.

Although the Racer has not confirmed his relationship with Mundt, fans speculate something is going on between the two. They were first spotted together in the paddock during the Tuscan Grand Prix of 2020.

Since then, the couple has been seen together on numerous occasions but has yet to make things Instagram official.

Mundt is currently studying Business Management and FInance.

George Russell Background

George William Russell is a British man who was born on 15 February 1998. At present, he is 23 years old.

As a child, George used to love karting. He started the sport at 7 years old after seeing his brother playing it.

He spends his youth at Wisbech Grammar School. At 18, he moved to Milton Keynes for his racing career.

George Russell Family And His Dad

George Russell is the son of Steve and Alison Russell. He has 2 siblings, Cara and Benjy.

His brother, Benjy, works in competitive karting. Although much information about his parents has not been reported, we know that they are incredibly supportive of his career, starting from his karting days.

The family resides in King’s Lynn, Norfolk, England.

George Russell Net Worth Revealed

The net worth of George Russell is estimated to be 1 million dollars. Currently, his salary is 958,000 dollars.

Russell earns his millions by racing formula1 cars for Williams.

Although he started his junior career by karting, he quickly moved on to racing in 2014. That year he made his debut in the Formula Renault 2.0 Alps CHampionships when he raced for Koiranen GP.

That same year he competed in Formula 4 championship. Although he didn’t get a significant ranking in the game, he got selected to join the British Racing Drivers’ Club SuperStars program.

The next year, he competed in the FIA European Formula 3 Championship, where he got the victory.

In 2019, he signed a contract with the Williams team to be an F1 driver. He got his first podium at the 2021 Belgian Grand Prix.
