All We Know About Italian Actress Marisela Federici

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, September 16, 2024

Learn about Marisela Federici in the article below. She is also the main protagonist in the novel Doa Bárbara.

Marisela Federici, also known as Rivas y Cardona is the president of Regina viarum as per the sources.

Moreover, she is a member of the Via Appia Antica Defense Group, as well as vice president of the AIRC (Italian Cancer Research Association) for Lazio and honorary vice president of the “Filarmonica Arturo Toscanini” association.

In addition, she is a member of the “Venice Foundation’s” Organizing Committee.

Marisela Federici is also the granddaughter of Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez and the wife of Paolo Federici.

Marisela Federici Wikipedia

The biography of Marisela Federici has yet to be added to Wikipedia.

She spent her youth in Madrid, where she and her family relocated following the events in Venezuela.

Carlos Delgado Chalbaud, her grandfather, was assassinated by dictator Marcos Pérez Jiménez.

After her marriage to Paolo Federici, a scion of a dynasty of Roman bankers and businessmen, who died in 2016, she became well-known in Italy.

Marisela is named after former Venezuelan president Rómulo Gallegos’ book, in which her mother went to beg while her husband was incarcerated for political reasons. Marisela was the name of the protagonist in the novel Doa Bárbara.

Marisela Federici Husband: Is She Married or Divorced?

Marisela Federici adopted the surname of her second husband, Paolo Federici, a well-known Roman businessman.

She became a widow after he left her with his son Eduardo (in 2016).

Marisela Federici was married to the famous Egyptian billionaire Roger Tamraz (creator of Tamoil) before her second wedding, with whom she produced her daughter Margherita Tamraz.

Due to his many betrayals, the marriage ended after seven years. We don’t know if Marisela has had any additional steady partnerships after her second husband died in 2016.
