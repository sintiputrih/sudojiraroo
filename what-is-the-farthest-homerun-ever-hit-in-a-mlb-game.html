The Legendary Home Runs

One of the most iconic names in baseball history, Babe Ruth, is credited with hitting a colossal 575-foot home run in 1921.

This historic feat, achieved during his tenure with the New York Yankees, solidifies Ruth's status as one of the greatest hitters of all time.
