Toni Braxton is as talented as she is gorgeous and you can see the singer/actress’ best looks here.

Toni Braxton, born October 7, 1967, is an American R&B singer and decorated performer. Toni rose to fame in the ‘90s, with hits like ‘Un-Break My Heart.’ She earned a number of Grammy Awards, including Best New Artist, and has amassed more than five Grammy Awards during the course of her career.

Toni is also a part of the famous musical family the Braxtons and has appeared in over 140 episodes of the series Braxton Family Values,’ which premiered in 2011. Toni has always been a mainstay in the music industry, performing at major events like the American Music Awards, the Super Bowl Halftime show, and so many more!
