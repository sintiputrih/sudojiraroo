Jessica Fern Missing: Where was she last seen?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, February 11, 2024

Jessica Fern is a Ridgefield, Washington State resident who went missing on August 15, 2023. Her disappearance has become a shock as people search for her all over. Her husband shared detailed information about her. In this post, we have more details about her. Keep reading for more.

Jessica Fern Missing: Where was she last seen?

Fern, a resident of Ridgefield, Washington State, has been reported missing since August 15, 2023. She departed from her residence driving a 2015 Black/Grey Ford Edge, wearing a flannel long-sleeve button-up shirt and black shorts. Following her disappearance, her family turned to social media to appeal for any information that could aid in locating her.

Fern’s spouse took to Facebook to share a missing person’s flyer, urging for assistance in finding his wife. She was last seen at their Ridgefield home. He also mentioned that on August 15, she left with their dog, Honzo, a Tan Irish wolfhound/German Shepherd mix. On August 16, her phone’s signal led authorities to Surprise Beach on the Vancouver, WA waterfront, where the dog was discovered. Regrettably, Jessica was not found in the vicinity of the beach.

Fern’s husband, who provided the license plate CCC7734 of the 2015 Black/Grey Ford Edge she was driving, requested that individuals contact the Ridgefield Police Department if they come across her. His post stated:

“Though her phone’s last signal came from Surprise Beach on the Vancouver, WA waterfront, the phone itself has not been located. Should you sight Jessica or possess any relevant information, kindly reach out to the Ridgefield Police Department at 360-887-3556 or contact me directly at 503-607-7300.”

On August 15, the family and friends of Jessica Fern resorted to social media in an appeal for aid in locating the Washington State native, who vanished after leaving her home on Tuesday evening.

In a separate Facebook post, her family unveiled that she departed in the company of their dog, subsequently discovered wandering near Surprise Beach on the Vancouver, WA waterfront the following day. In an updated post, her husband, Daniel Fern, conveyed:

“Animal patrol retrieved our dog Honzo from the vicinity close to where Jessica’s phone was last traced – Surprise Beach. Our relentless search for Jessica continues.”

Furthermore, Jessica Fern’s husband put out a call for volunteers to assist in the search for his missing wife. Daniel Fern invited those willing to participate to gather at the common area between Wild Fin Grill and Twigs Bistro on the Vancouver, WA waterfront on August 17.

“Volunteers Needed: We extend our heartfelt gratitude for the overwhelming support we’ve received. Many have expressed their desire to contribute. Tomorrow, 8/17, at 11 AM, I intend to distribute additional missing person flyers. Let’s meet between Wild Fin Grill and Twigs Bistro at the common area, Vancouver, WA waterfront. Please signal your intent to participate,” he composed.
