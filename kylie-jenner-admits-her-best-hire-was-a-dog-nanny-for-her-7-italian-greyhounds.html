Kylie Jenner Admits Her Best Hire Was a Dog Nanny for Her 7 Italian Greyhounds

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, May 8, 2024

Now only 26 years old, Kylie Jenner has had a packed schedule since she was a teen. From being a reality TV icon, model, makeup brand founded,r to being named the Youngest Self-Made Billionaire by Forbes in 2019, she’s not slowing down. But she doesn’t do it all without a bit of help – Including assistants, stylists, trainers, and more.

But when recently being interviewed by Jennifer Lawrence for Interview Magazine, Lawrence asked, “Okay, little mogul. What is the best hire you’ve ever made?” and Kylie answered, “Probably my dog nanny, since I have seven dogs.” And when asked, “Why seven?” – Kylie laughs and says, “Because they kept multiplying. One of them had children and I had to keep the family together.” This is a reference to her two Italian Greyhounds, Norman and Bambi, having two puppies back in 2016, who Kylie kept and named Rosy and Harley. Lawrence went on to joke that, “Do you think that you have greyhound dogs because they remind you of Kendall?” Kylie replied, “Yes, that’s a part of it. I love seeing little Kendalls run around, and I think they’re just funny. They just make me smile. I was never, like, a Shih Tzu, Pomeranian type of girl.”

And when Kylie refers to her seven dogs, she’s actually referring to her seven Italian Greyhounds *inhale* Bambi, Norman, Rosy, Harley, Sophia, Kevin, and Jill *exhale*. While small, these little doggos are known for their need for speed. So, since she can afford it, it’s great she got them a nanny to ensure they get all the exercise and attention they need.
