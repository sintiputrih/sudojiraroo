7 Jeans to Snag on Amazon From $12, Including a Priyanka Chopra-Worn Pair

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, May 5, 2024

There are a few style staples that are non-negotiables in any fall wardrobe: chunky sweaters, snuggly loungewear, and — of course — classic jeans. While denim is a year-round must-have, a great pair of jeans is essential at this time of year more than any. Luckily, Amazon is overflowing with high-quality denim at shockingly budget-friendly costs — no really, prices start at just $12. 

To narrow down the retailer’s impressive selection, I sifted through thousands of pairs and found the eight styles shoppers love most. The following picks are all pulled from Amazon’s best-sellers chart, meaning they’re clear customer favorites. You may want to snag the pairs that catch your eye while they’re still on sale for up to 75 percent off — they’re trending for a reason and they may sell out quickly. 

Best-Selling Jeans at Amazon: 

Gloria Vanderbilt Amanda Jeans

You can’t beat $12 jeans; the Gloria Vanderbilt Amanda pair is currently an entire 75 percent off its typical $48 price tag. If their incredible markdown isn’t reason enough to snag the jeans, they also happen to be Amazon’s number one best-selling style — more than 5,000 people bought them in the past month, according to the retailer. And shoppers aren’t the only fans of the timeless style, Priyanka Chopra said she “can spend all day” in Gloria Vanderbilt jeans. Browse through the pair’s range of washes at Amazon, and be sure to snag your favorites while they’re still less than $15. 

Levi's Women's Low Pro Jeans

Speaking of pairs with celebrity stamps of approval, the Levi’s Low Pro jeans that Kylie Jenner wears are now half-off. The flattering style is designed with a low-rise waistband, classic bootcut silhouette, and comfortable fit through the hip and thigh. The jeans are available in 11 washes and distress levels to pair perfectly with practically anything in your autumn outfit rotation. If you’re still not sold, take it from one reviewer who called them the “best jeans ever” since they “hug in all the right places” yet maintain a “relaxed look.”   

Lee Monroe Straight Leg Jeans

Looking for the perfect, classic jeans to add to your denim collection? The Lee Monroe Straight Leg Jeans are just that — and they’re an entire 57 percent off. At just $18, the pair can easily be elevated with a chic blouse or dressed down with your go-to sweater. As one shopper put it, the pants are “so versatile” and “go with just about anything.” And a second reviewer added that they're “the most comfortable pair” they’ve “ever worn.”  

Head to Amazon to shop the most popular jeans at Amazon while prices still start at just $12.
