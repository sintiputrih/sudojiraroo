Bill Goldberg is a professional wrestler and former NFL football player who was born on December 27, 1966 in Tulsa, OK. He is best known for his time in the WCW and WWE, where he held the WCW World Heavyweight Championship and the World Heavyweight Championship. He is considered one of the most successful wrestlers of all time.
