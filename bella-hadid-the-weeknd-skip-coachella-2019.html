Bella Hadid &amp; The Weeknd Share Photos from Idyllic Trip Together During Coachella 2019

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 22, 2024
  • As Coachella 2019 kicks off, Bella Hadid and her boyfriend, The Weeknd, have escaped on a romantic vacation together.
  • Sharing a mirror selfie featuring The Weeknd, Hadid wrote "MIA on purpose" on Instagram.
  • From riding jet skis, to admiring idyllic sunsets, the power couple has been savoring some quiet time away from the craziness of festival season.

Coachella 2019 might have just kicked off, but Bella Hadid and The Weeknd, real name Abel Tesfaye, are on a romantic vacation elsewhere.

Sharing a series of photos and videos on Instagram, Hadid gave the world an insight into her rekindled romance with musician Tesfaye. In one post, Hadid wrote, "MIA on purpose," presumably because both celebs are missing Coachella Weekend 1 right now. Hadid and Tesfaye sparked reunion rumors in April 2018 when they were spotted kissing at a Coachella after party. Since then, they've become inseparable, and have traveled worldwide together, from having chic dates in Paris, to vacations in Tokyo, Japan. They also attended Kylie Jenner's 21st birthday in August 2018 as a couple.
