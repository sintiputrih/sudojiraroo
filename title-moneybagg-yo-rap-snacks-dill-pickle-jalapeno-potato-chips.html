Moneybagg Yo Gets His Own Dill Pickle Jalapeo Potato Chips

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, March 29, 2024

After launching his own water brand called Vior Water in January, Moneybagg Yo has now secured a snack to go along with his new beverage.

In a video posted to Instagram on Tuesday (March 29), the Memphis rapper can be seen passing around a cardboard box filled with Rap Snacks Dill Pickle Jalapeño Chips with his own face on the bag.

“Pack just dropped, man,” a voice can be heard saying as Moneybagg’s friends each take a bag.

Moneybagg Yo has been adamant about getting a foot in the snack game. In January, he shared footage of his Vior Water bottles already being made on the factory lines.

“100 million mood @viorwater,” Moneybagg captioned the clips. “Send me ya address I’ll send ya a case and put ya down I’m telling u now this the best water in America.”

In 2020, the “Wokesha” rapper also tweeted out he had snagged a deal to create his own brand of chip-like snacks called Crunchies.

“Just Close Deal Out Wit Rap Snacks Let Me kno Wat Flavors Y’all Want GO!!” he wrote after announcing the deal on social media.

Moneybagg Yo now joins the ranks of Cardi B, Lil Baby,Boosie Badazz, Migos, Rick Ross, Fabolous and Lil Yachty, who all have their own chip flavors as well.

However, he revealed he’s not stopping at snacks. During an interview with Power 105.1 in November, Moneybagg revealed plans to release his own clothing line, weed brand and even his own feature-length film.
