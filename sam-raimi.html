Samuel M. Raimi is an American film director, producer, screenwriter, and actor. He is known for his movies and TV series, such as; ‘Evil Dead’ Horror series, and others. Among directorial, he is better known for the ‘Spider Man’ trilogy aired from 2002 to 2007.

Among other notable film directorial credits, the 90s’ superhero film ‘Darkman’, ‘crime thriller film titled ‘A Simple Plan’ (1998), ‘The Gift’ (2000), and many others that are on the hit list.

Career

Throughout his career, Samuel M. Raimi earned immense popularity and praise from the viewers for his marvelous performances. He is known for his breath-taking performances and wonderful acting prowess which make him stand out.

Raimi made his career in both the film and television industry and made numerous appearances in movies and television series. He has been working in movies and TV series since 1977. It was the year when he debuted as a writer, director, and producer through a film named ‘It’s Murder’. In the film, he acted himself and got acclaimed.

He then didn’t look back and continued working in the film and TV industry. He has worked on more than 20 movies, 24 TV series (as a producer), 15 short films, 7 TV series, *as a director, producer, writer), 18 TV series, short movies (as an executive producer only), and 27 movies as an actor.

Raimi has made his wider career by working on many projects as an actor, director, producer, director, and writer. And what’s more? He won many awards for his work.

Achievements

Among many prestigious awards, Raimi won the Sitges – Catalan International Film Festival Prize of the International Critics’ Jury 1981.
