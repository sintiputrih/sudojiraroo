Carol Hagen Biography

Hagen was born in 1959 in Seattle, WA, US. She will be around 62 to 63 years in 2022. Information about her early life and career is out of the public. However, she began her career first as a flight attendant. Later, she switched to her current field as a real estate agent.

Carol Hagen’s Marriage To Lester Holt

Lester met his current wife back in the 1980s when she was working as a flight attendant. They started dating for some time before marrying on 8 May 1982. The couple had been married for over 40 years now. They are also blessed with two children namely Stefan and Cameron.
