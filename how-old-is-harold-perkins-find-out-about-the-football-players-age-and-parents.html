How Old Is Harold Perkins? Find Out About The Football Players Age And Parents

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, January 11, 2024

If you follow American football, you must be familiar with Harold Perkins. The player was one of the top drafts picks of 2022, and fans are curious to know about the player. Here is everything you want to know about the player, including his age and parents.

The player signed a contract with LSU on 2 February 2022. Initially, the player was committed to Texas A&M. Later, the player decided to de-commit from Texas.

The sports analysts are considering his recruitment to LSU as one of the most interesting signings of 2022. Let’s see how he performs for LSU in upcoming games.

Harold Perkins Age And Height: How Old And Tall Is He?

Harold Perkins has made a lot of progression at a very young age. The player stands at the height of 6 feet 3 inches.

Perkins is one of the top-rated prospects for the future and is ranked No.4 for the entire class of 2022 by ESPN. According to 247 Sports, the player is the nation’s No.8 overall prospect and the No.2 linebacker in the country.

A powerful and dynamic athlete, Perkins collected 983 yards and 14 touchdowns on 96 carries in just ten games in his junior season. LSU will be looking to play him in the outside defensive line.

 

Harold Perkins Bio: Is He On Wikipedia?

Harold Perkins’s bio is available on different sports and media websites, but the player is yet to have a separate profile on the official Wikipedia page.

The player started his career as a running back in high school. However, the player got into the first team of All-Greater Houston as a utility player on offense. He also took participated in the Under Armour All-America Game.

The LSU linebacker is native to New Orleans. However, he played his college football for Cypress Park in Cypress, Texas. Later, he moved to Houston, where he played basketball.

Harold Perkins Parents Revealed 

Harold Perkins’s parents are highly proud of their son’s achievement. His mother, Bertha Walton, has always been the source of inspiration to him.

His mother moved to Houston when he was two years old. Perkins never loved Houston, but later, he realized why his mother moved there. In Houston, he got exposure to bigger opportunities, making him one of the best athletes.

Perkins was raised with his four siblings. His older brother, Perky, also played football in high school, but he ended his journey after high school. So, he never wanted Perkins to do the same and always motivated him to train and play.
