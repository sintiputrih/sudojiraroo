Parents

Love Story Of Her Parents

Pep Guardiola was only eighteen-year-old when he met the love of his life. Her name is Cristina Serra. 

The couple first met each other in an exclusive clothing store in Barcelona. 

The Serra family-owned that clothing store where they both met. 

Pep Guardiola was invited there to do some modeling for the designer Antonio Miro. 

That’s how their love story began, the couple had long term relationshipsbefore they tied the knot. 

The pair got married in the year 2014 with the presences of very close family and best friend at Catalonia. 

It was a very low-key affair. 

Now the couple is blessed with three children, including two girls and one boy. 

Their names are Maria Guardiola, Valentina Guardiola, and Marius Guardiola.

Recently the family suffered a huge loss. Her loving grandmother (Dolors Sala) just passed away on April 6th, 2020, because of coronavirus complications. 

It was a really tough time for the family.
