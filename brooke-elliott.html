Who is Brooke Elliott?

Brooke Elliott is a singer and actress from the United States. She is well recognized for her role as Jane Bingum in Drop Dead Diva, which she starred in from 2009 through 2014.

She received a Gracie Award in 2010 and a Women’s Image Network Award in 2011. In addition, as a director and writer, she completed a short TV film titled Say Hello in 2016.

Brooke Elliott’s Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, and Educational Background

According to her academic records, she first attended Gabriel Richard High School and then Western Michigan University. She graduated from the latter in 1998 with a degree in Musical Theatre Performance.

Brooke Elliott’s Professional Life and Career

Brooke Elliott has become a well-known figure in the acting world, appearing in several films. She is currently occupied in a TV drama, Sweet Magnolias, in which she plays Dana Sue Sullivan.
