Wander Franco Wife Revealed: Love and Allegations!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, January 16, 2024

Wander Franco’s wife’s name has been revealed for the first time. This comes as the Tampa Bay Rays star is being accused of having inappropriate relationships with several girls under the age of 18.

Franco had not said who his wife is before Friday, when The Daily Mail reported that he is married to Rachelly Paulino, who is 21 and has been his childhood friend since they lived in the Dominican Republic together.

According to our research, Franco and Paulino got married after the 2021 season and now have two children together. Given the latest accusations, it’s hard to say what Franco’s relationship with Paulino is like right now.

Franco is a star player for the Rays. In his third year in MLB, he was named an All-Star. He has been put on the restricted list and is taking a break from the team.

This comes after the league started looking into the shortstop after finding disturbing things about him on social media. Last week, these posts came out, and then there were accusations.

“We support any steps taken by the league to better understand the situation,” the Rays said in a statement.

“Out of respect for all parties involved, we have no further comments at this time.”

The posts, which were supposedly popular on X before they were taken down, said that Franco was having an inappropriate connection with a minor.

Also, a 17-year-old girl is said to have filed a charge against Franco in the Dominican Republic, according to the newspaper Diario Libre.

The Dominican Republic’s attorney general’s office has also told the Associated Press that they are looking into the situation.

People say that both Franco and Paulino grew up in Bani, Dominican Republic, and that they have known each other since at least 2016.
