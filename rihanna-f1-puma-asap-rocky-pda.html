Rihanna Attends F1 x Puma Launch in the Coolest Leather Jacket

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, June 15, 2024

Rihanna oozed cool glam while supporting beau A$AP Rocky at his Puma x F1 collection launch Friday night.

The “Work It” singer stepped out in an edgy dark brown leather maxi jacket, featuring a structured collar, silver hardware, many decorative pockets, silver jeweled detailing on one shoulder, and a patchwork style design. She dressed things up with a pair of black open-toe pumps and lots of bling, including diamond stud earrings by De Beers, a collection of sparkly silver necklaces, and an anklet on her left ankle. She rounded off the look with a high, slicked back top knot, fluttery eyelashes, a glossy brown lip, and her neon green nails.

The “Praise the Lord” rapper, who is the creative director of Puma x F1, walked alongside Riri, and the two held hands. He repped pieces from the debuted collection, including neon yellow gloves, baggy blue pants with white stitching and the brand’s logo across the front, and a gray jacket with a red collar and green lining over a graphic tee. He accented his Formula 1 style with a seatbelt-style belt, neon yellow shoes, and a black baseball cap that read “ALLAH FACTORY RACING” across the front.

The couple began dating in 2021 and share two sons, one-year old RZA and three-month-old Riot Rose.

When asked about the best thing he and Rihanna have collaborated on, A$AP answered: their children. “We do a real great job at collaborating and making children. I think that’s our best creation so far. Nothing is better than that out there, any design…We had these beautiful angels, so that’s the best collaboration,” he told ComplexMusic.
