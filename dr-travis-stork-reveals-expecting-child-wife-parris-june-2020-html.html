Dr. Travis Stork reveals he is expecting his first child with wife Parris in June 2020

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, April 23, 2024

Dr. Travis has revealed he is expecting his first child with wife Parris in June 2020.   

Stork, 47, and his wife Parris (nee Bell), 27, tied the knot in an intimate ceremony officiated by their friend in Nashville.

The happy couple shared the news with a number of colorful photos on their respective Instagram pages, complete with their own hashtag, #Storkbaby.
