Guy Ritchie Net Worth

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 8, 2024

English film director, Guy Ritchie, has a net worth of about $150 million.

Ritchie who began working in the movie industry at an early age is best known for the gangster and the Sherlock Holmes films.

He made his directorial debut in 1995 with the short film, The Hard Case.

He went ahead to direct other movies such as Lock, Stock and Two Smoking Barrels (1998), Snatch (2000), Swept Away (2002), Revolver (2005), RocknRolla (2008), Sherlock Holmes and its sequel, Sherlock Holmes: A Game of Shadows.

Other films directed by him include The Man From U.N.C.L.E. (2015), King Arthur: Legend of the Sword (2017), Aladdin (2019), The Gentlemen (2019), and Wrath of Man.

Ritchie is an Academy Award nominee. He was nominated for Best Original Score and Best Art Direction for the movie Sherlock Holmes.

He made over $1 billion from the movie Aladdin, with a gross ranking of number 34 across the globe.

Does Guy Ritchie Appear In His Films?

Ritchie has appeared in some of his films like Mean Machine, Blokes, Birds, and Backenders, I’m Going to Tell You a Secret, How Bruce Lee Changed the World, and Making ‘Snatch’.
