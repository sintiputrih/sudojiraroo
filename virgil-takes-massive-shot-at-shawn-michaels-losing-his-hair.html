Virgil Takes Massive Shot At Shawn Michaels Losing His Hair

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, February 28, 2024

WWE Hall Of Famer Shawn Michaels is definitely one of the best in-ring performers to have ever graced WWE ring. He was touted as Mr. WrestleMania and he truly lived up to the moniker. It seems Virgil recently trolled him once again.

Shawn Michaels has always exceeded expectations when it comes to performing in a match. Many say that he was so good inside the ring, that Michaels could even have a believable match with a broomstick.

Virgil has not worked in WWE for several years now. Despite that, he has managed to keep himself relevant thanks to a series of hilarious social media posts. It is no secret he is not a fan of Shawn Michaels either.

Back in the 90s Shawn Michaels had long locks of hair which he would flaunt at every opportunity. However, nowadays Michaels is sporting a more bald look, as his hair isn’t what it used to be. Virgil took to his Instagram story and took a huge shot at Michaels’ balding head.

Shawn Michaels is currently busy running NXT 2.0 while Triple H is currently recovering from a previous heart-related incident. Michaels has simply accepted that he won’t get his hair back anymore and that is the end of that.
